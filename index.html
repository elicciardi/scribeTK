                📋 Sgarbossa Criteria (LBBB / Paced rhythm)
            
            
    
                
    Use when LBBB or ventricular-paced rhythm obscures normal STEMI criteria.
    
                
                
    Original Sgarbossa (score ≥3 = positive):
    
                
      
                    
    • 5 pts: Concordant ST elevation ≥1 mm in any lead (ST↑ in same direction as QRS)
      • 
                    
    • 3 pts: Concordant ST depression ≥1 mm in V1, V2, or V3
      • 
                    
    • 2 pts: Excessively discordant ST elevation ≥5 mm in a lead with negative QRS
      • 
                
    
                
                
    Modified Sgarbossa (Smith) — any one = positive:
    
                
      
                    
    • Concordant ST elevation ≥1 mm in any lead
      • 
                    
    • Concordant ST depression ≥1 mm in V1–V3
      • 
                    
    • Discordant ST elevation with ST/S ratio ≥ 0.25 (more sensitive than 5 mm rule)
      • 
                
    
                
    Any positive criterion → consider acute MI / STEMI alert.
